Elephant men

June 1, 2006 BY Piero Macchioni

Mentre in Gran Bretagna il leder conservatore David Cameron viene accusato di voler candidare gente troppo fighetta e assai benestante (gli “chichi”) la Nationa Review, negli Usa, compila la classifica delle 50 canzoni indispensabili per un conservatore. Ecco la top-ten:

1. “Won’t Get Fooled Again,” by The Who 2. “Taxman,” by The Beatles 3. “Sympathy for the Devil,” by The Rolling Stones 4. “Sweet Home Alabama,” by Lynyrd Skynyrd 5. “Wouldn’t It Be Nice,” by The Beach Boys **6. **“Gloria,” by U2 7. “Revolution,” by The Beatles 8. “Bodies,” by The Sex Pistols 9. “Don’t Tread on Me,” by Metallica 10. “20th Century Man,” by The Kinks
