What will matter in the next age of media

        April 26, 2016
                
The Joshua Topolsky’s grocery list of what will matter in the next age of media. Compelling voices and stories, real and raw talent, new ideas that actually serve or delight an audience, brands that have meaning and ballast; these are things that matter in the next age of media. Thinking of your platform as an actual platform, not a delivery method. Knowing you’re more than just your words.
