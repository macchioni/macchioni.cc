Il complotto contro Israele

        July 31, 2006
                

Di questi tempi si moltiplicano gli articoli che mettono in discussione Israele. Uno che sta facendo il giro del mondo via blog è questo di William Hughes: «What If Israel Had Never Been Created?». Lui ha trovato 25 motivi, come:

  1. The U.S. would not have any enemies in the Islamic World.

  1. There would be no Al-Qaeda Terrorist Network.

  2. Gasoline would be selling for less than $1 a gallon.

  3. There would have been no 9⁄11.

  4. There would be no USA Patriot Law.

  5. There would be no Homeland Security Agency.

  6. The Israeli Lobby’s “unmatched power” over the U.S.’ foreign policy, for over four decades, would not had existed.

  7. There would also not have been any Neocon ideologues; like Paul Wolfowitz, William Kristol, Richard Perle, et al, to help, (along with other “Special Interests”), to push the U.S. into an illegal war with Iraq.

Would Winnie-the-Pooh vote Conservative? In a Commons motion to congratulate Pooh on his 80th birthday, Andrew Rosindell — a traditional roast beef sort of Tory MP — praises his “fine values”. But Pooh is really more of a modern, compassionate Conservative. Not only did he welcome immigrants to the Hundred Acre Wood (Tigger, Kanga and Baby Roo) and build a house for homeless Eeyore, but he led a celebrated expotition to the North Pole where he’ll have seen for himself the effects of global warming.
