Bravo 'sto Sofocle

        August 26, 2009
                
“Oedipus Rex”—Average reader rating: Four stars. Sophocles is a satisfying author who writes in clear, snappy prose. Youngsters in particular could learn a lot by imitating Mr. Rex, until he goes a bit off the rails toward the end. Nothing earth-shattering here, but zippy stuff. Have to admit I’m still puzzled by the weird subplot involving Mr. Rexs mother. I grandi classici raccontati come se fossero stati recensiti dagli utenti di Amazon.
