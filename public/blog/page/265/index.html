Elogio dell'uomo vanitoso

        September 21, 2005
                
Il deputato conservatore e biondastro Boris Johnson ha scritto che gli uomini sono molto piu vanitosi delle donne. Pero in fondo va bene perche aiuta a compiere grandi imprese. Asked to give a candid self-assessment, women and girls will stick to “good” or “average” while men of identical abilities will declare that they are “very good” or “excellent.” Girls are much more reluctant to put up their hands and show off in class, and yet do as well, if not better in exams.
