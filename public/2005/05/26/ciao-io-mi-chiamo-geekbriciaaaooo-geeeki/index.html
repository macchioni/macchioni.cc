Ciao, io mi chiamo geek<br><i>Ciaaaooo geeek</i>

May 26, 2005 by Piero Macchioni

Maniaci del web, voi che la prima cosa che fate al rientro a casa e controllare la posta elettronica, ecco i primi quattro dei 12 passi per guarire da questa malattia:

1) I will have a cup of coffee in the morning and read my PAPER newspaper like I used to, before the Web.

2) I will eat breakfast with a knife and fork and not with one hand typing.

3) I will get dressed before noon.

4) I will make an attempt to clean the house, wash clothes, and plan dinner before even thinking of the Web.
